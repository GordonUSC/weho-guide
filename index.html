The corridor

It all happens on Santa Monica Blvd.

Boystown is the stretch of Santa Monica Boulevard between roughly Robertson and La Cienega. Bars, patios, dance floors, and drag, all walkable, all shoulder to shoulder. Start anywhere, drift down the block, and let the night find you.

The season

Pride lives here.

West Hollywood is home to one of the world's biggest Pride celebrations, a June run of a music festival, rallies, and community fairs. The headline WeHo Pride weekend is typically early June, so the big parade has likely just passed for 2026, but Pride energy lingers on the strip all month.
